Volunteers Needed

Are you a compassionate communicator with a heart for ministry and healing?

Daughters Who Speak is seeking dedicated volunteers to serve as Voice Ambassadors—individuals who extend the love of Christ through meaningful follow-up calls to women impacted by emotional, spiritual, or sexual abuse in faith-based spaces.

As a Voice Ambassador, you will be the caring voice that reminds women they are not alone. You’ll help us cultivate a culture of ongoing support, spiritual encouragement, and connection.
