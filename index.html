Mission

To support and empower women who have experienced abuse at the hands of spiritual leaders by providing confidential support, trauma-informed resources, and advocacy for healing and justice

R.E.A.C.H. – A Healing Journey

Mark 5:24–34

What We Offer

Confidential Support

Safe spaces to share your story and find encouragement from others who understand.

Trauma-Informed Resources

Counseling referrals, support groups, and educational tools for healing.

Advocacy & Justice

Guidance on reporting abuse, finding legal aid, and navigating difficult spiritual dynamics.

Faith & Recovery Tools

Support for women working through the spiritual confusion, grief, or loss caused by abuse within the church.
