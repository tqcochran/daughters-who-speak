About Us

We support women who’ve experienced spiritual abuse by religious leaders. Through community, resources, and advocacy, we help survivors reclaim their voice and walk in healing.

R.E.A.C.H. – A Healing Journey

Mark 5:24–34

R – Recognize the truth of what happened.

E – Empowered by God's Word.

A – Align with God’s truth and love

C – Connect with safe support and community

H – Heal through faith, forgiveness, and forward movement
